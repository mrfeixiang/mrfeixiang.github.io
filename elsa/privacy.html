Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

ElsaSnowMagic ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal data collection: We do not collect names, emails, or any identifying information.
  • No camera data storage: Hand tracking data is processed locally on your device in real-time and is never stored or transmitted.
  • No analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics services.
  • No advertising: The App contains no advertisements.

3. Hand Tracking Data

The App uses Apple Vision Pro's hand tracking feature to detect hand gestures. This data:

  • Is processed entirely on your device
  • Is never recorded, stored, or transmitted
  • Is used only to trigger visual effects in real-time
  • Cannot be accessed by us or any third party

4. Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on our website.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
mrfeixiang@gmail.com

By using ElsaSnowMagic, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

隐私政策

最后更新：2026年2月

1. 简介

ElsaSnowMagic（以下简称"本应用"）尊重您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们如何处理您使用本应用时的信息。

2. 我们收集的信息

我们不收集、存储或传输任何个人信息。具体而言：

  • 不收集个人数据：我们不收集姓名、电子邮件或任何身份识别信息。
  • 不存储摄像头数据：手部追踪数据仅在您的设备上实时处理，从不存储或传输。
  • 不使用分析工具：我们不使用任何第三方分析服务。
  • 无广告：本应用不包含任何广告。

3. 手部追踪数据

本应用使用 Apple Vision Pro 的手部追踪功能来检测手势。此数据：

  • 完全在您的设备上处理
  • 从不被记录、存储或传输
  • 仅用于实时触发视觉效果
  • 我们或任何第三方无法访问

4. 儿童隐私

本应用不收集任何人的信息，包括13岁以下的儿童。

5. 政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何变更将在应用内或我们的网站上公布。

6. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过以下方式联系我们：
