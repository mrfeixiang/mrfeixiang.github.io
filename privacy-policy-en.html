Privacy Policy

Lingqi Divination (灵棋经占卜)
Last Updated: January 22, 2026

Important Notice: We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using this app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection

We collect the following types of information:

  • Divination Questions: The text of questions you input for divination purposes
  • Divination Results: Generated hexagrams and AI interpretations
  • History Records: Your divination history stored locally on your device
  • Language Preferences: Your chosen interface language settings
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and other technical information for app performance improvement

2. Information Usage

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: Process your divination requests and generate AI interpretations
  • Experience Improvement: Optimize app features and user interface
  • Technical Support: Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Statistical Analysis: Understand app usage patterns to improve service quality

Our Commitment: Your divination questions and results will not be shared with third parties and are used solely to provide divination services to you.

3. Data Storage

Local Storage: Your history records and preference settings are stored on your device, which we cannot access.

Cloud Processing: Divination requests are sent to our servers for AI processing, but your personal questions are not permanently stored.

Data Retention: Temporary processing data is deleted immediately after service completion and is not retained long-term.

4. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data security:

  • Encrypted Transmission: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Server data employs industry-standard encryption technology
  • Access Control: Strict limitation of data access permissions
  • Regular Audits: Regular security audits and vulnerability scans

5. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • AI Service Provider: Used to generate divination interpretations (data is anonymized)
  • Analytics Service: Used to track app usage statistics (does not include personally identifiable information)

These third-party service providers all comply with strict privacy protection standards.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.
